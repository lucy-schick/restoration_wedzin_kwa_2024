5 Recomendations

  • Conduct an analysis of low flow periods at station 08EE003 located near Houston to evaluate the trend of flow volumes over the period of record. A declining trend was noted between 1930 and 2000 at the Bulkley River at Quick (station #08EE002) by A. Gottesfeld and Rabnett (2007b).
  • Conduct field assessments at all past restoration sites (HWI, MWMT, WFN, FIA, etc.) using pre-determined monitoring criteria to evaluate effectiveness of the work for achieving stated or inferred project objectives. Watershed Restoration Procedures - Routine effectiveness evaluation procedures and riparian inventory techniques (LMH25 - percent cover by structural stage, species composition, etc.) utilizing pre-built forms in the collaborative GIS project.
